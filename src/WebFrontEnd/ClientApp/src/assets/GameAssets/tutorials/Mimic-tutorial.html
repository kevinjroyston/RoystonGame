Mimic

See the picture? Mimic the picture.

Stage 1 - Draw Something

You will be given a short period of time to draw something.

Stage 2 - Study the Drawing

The drawing will appear on the shared game viewer (not your device).

Stage 3 - Mimic

Do your best to recreate the drawing you just saw.

Stage 4 - Find the Original

Everyone's drawings will appear on the shared game viewer.   They start off blurred and slowly come into focus.  As soon as you can identify the correct drawing, select the number on your device.

Points

You get points for choosing the correct drawing.  You get more points the faster you find it.

You also earn points if other players choose your drawing, thinking it was the original drawing.
